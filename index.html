Taichi Yagami

A second-year student who goes to Tsukishima General High School. He belongs to the soccer club, and he practices hard every day. Taichi is in the same class as Sora Takenouchi. He served as the leader of the DigiDestined during the adventure into the Digital World six years ago. His strong sense of justice and his straightforward earnest side haven't changed since elementary school, but lately he's also been worrying about where he's headed in life.

Agumon

Taichi's partner Digimon; he's a reptile Digimon. He usually has a carefree personality, but when the time comes, he'll stand up to enemies regardless of the danger. His special attack is Baby Flame, in which he spits a fireball from his mouth.

Yamato Ishida

A second-year student at Tsukishima General High School. Yamato's enrolled in the class next to Taichi's and Sora's class. At a glance he looks calm and collected, but the truth is he's warm-hearted and thoughtful of his friends. That straightforwardness sometimes leads to his opinions clashing with Taichi's. Yamato's been in a band since junior high, playing bass and singing vocals. Perhaps due to musical differences with the other members, Teen-Age Wolves has temporarily broken up. He recently formed a new band.

Gabumon

Yamato's partner Digimon; while he wears a Garurumon pelt, he's actually a reptile Digimon like Agumon. His special attack is Petit Fire, in which he attacks using small balls of flame

Sora Takenouchi

A second-year student at Tsukishima General High School, Sora's a childhood friend of Taichi's and in the same class as him. She has a lively, openhearted personality, but she also possesses the kindness and sensitivity to be considerate of others, along with a strong sense of responsibility. Sora is something of a "big sister" figure that keeps the peace within the group, such as when she attempts to mediate the occasional clashes between Taichi and Yamato.

Biyomon

Sora's partner Digimon; a small bird Digimon with wings that have grown like arms. Her special attack is Magical Fire, in which she attacks using phantom flames.

Koshiro Izumi

A first-year student at Tsukishima General High School. Koshiro is an intellectual and a theorist, and he knows a lot about things like computers and the Internet. He excels at analysis, and at grasping situations. Koshiro has an active side, as seen in the way he communicates online with DigiDestined all over the world, but he also has a tendency to get too wrapped up in the gathering of knowledge and information, thus making him lose sight of his surroundings.

Tentomon

Koshiro's partner Digimon; an Insectoid Digimon, with a hard shell and six limbs. His special attack is Petit Thunder, in which he emits static electricity from his antennae. For some reason he speaks using the Kansai dialect.

Mimi Tachikawa

Mimi has a cheerful, innocent personality. She does things at her own pace and can be selfish, but she's fundamentally a kind girl whom everyone likes. She likes singing and is good at it, but she has peculiar tastes in music, due in part to influence from her parents. Mimi was living in America because of her parents' jobs, but now she's returned to Japan. Living abroad for a long time has taken the edge off her na√Øve nature.

Palmon

Mimi's partner Digimon; a Vegetation Digimon with a tropical flower attached to her head. Her special attack is Poison Ivy, in which she attacks with poisonous ivy.

Joe Kido

Joe has a steady, serious personality. As a third-year student who goes to a college prep high school in the Tokyo Metropolitan area, he's studying to get into medical school. Joe's getting ready for college entrance exams and also attending cram school as he studies hard, but his grades aren't improving the way he'd like, and it's starting to worry him.

Gomamon

Joe's partner Digimon; a sea animal type Digimon that's covered in warm fur and good at swimming. When he gets angry, he makes the red fur from his head down to his back bristle. His special attack is Marching Fishes, in which he freely controls his fish followers.

Takeru Takaishi

A second-year student who goes to Odaiba Junior High School. He lives apart from his older brother Yamato due to their parents' divorce, but they get along well with each other, and they frequently keep in touch. Like Koushiro, Takeru keeps in touch with DigiDestined around the world by way of blogs.

Patamon

Takeru's partner Digimon; a mammal type Digimon with distinctively large ears. His special attack is Air Shot, in which he blows out all the air he's inhaled

Hikari Yagami

Taichi's sister, a second-year student who goes to Odaiba Junior High School. She has a kind personality and is constantly worried about her big brother Taichi, but she's also known to be strong to the core. She loves sweet foods, and convenience store ice cream has been her favorite lately. Hikari developed an interest in photography during elementary school, and she still pursues it to this day.

Gatomon

Hikari's partner Digimon; an Animal Digimon that looks like a kitten. Her special attack is Cat Punch, in which she attacks using long sharp claws.
